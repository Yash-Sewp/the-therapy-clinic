“The Therapy Clinic” is a Multidisciplinary Practice which was established in Gauteng in 2000. The focus is on qualitative intervention to ensure optimal functioning of both children and adults. A strong emphasis is placed on the quality of intervention which is navigated by skilled assessment, insightful clinical knowledge/analysis and specific goal-directed therapy. This is supported by regular parent support and contact with the other relevant professionals.

The Occupational Therapist is trained in Sensory Integration and Neurodevelopmental Therapy (NDT). Her work experience spans both public and private hospitals and schools. She is also dual registered and is a Registered Trauma Counsellor working with both children and adults.
