About

This is a private practice that offers Trauma Counselling, Grief Counselling, Counselling for Depression, Marriage Counselling, Child and Teenage Counselling and Family Counselling.

My philosophy is that mental wellbeing is an insightful journey of introspection, reflection and application, to achieve the goal of happiness and stability.

I have been in practice for 10 years, working with children, teenagers, and adults . Parent counselling has also formed a large component of the practice.
